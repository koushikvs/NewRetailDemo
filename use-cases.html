Coastline Athletics · Spring '26 Summit Trail Capsule
Coastline Athletics — a 412-store national sporting-goods chain — places a Spring '26 pre-book PO for the Summit Trail Capsule: a coordinated 6-SKU collection (jacket, pant, two tee colours, headband, gloves) that has to land complete on a fixed in-store launch date of April 12. Collection-master rules in S/4 say all six SKUs are mandatory inclusions for the capsule drop — partial = no drop.

Stock Protection Transparency
AI Moment 1 of 2 Live
Today
For a curated collection drop, when even one SKU is short the buyer's whole capsule lands incomplete — and partial drops are scorecard penalties at a Tier-1 retailer. Today, ops finds out from the warehouse the day of pick-pack: emails fly, the buyer gets called, the capsule slips. Sometimes the missing supply is sitting unallocated in another channel — but the gap is invisible.
With AI
The agent reads aATP's PAC result across all 6 SKUs in the collection, identifies the allocation drift, cross-references DTC channel buffers for unallocated supply, and proposes a channel-buffer rebalance that ships the capsule complete. Every rebalance posts to the immutable audit ledger — no opaque inventory moves.
↓ Hours of ops escalation → 4 minutes. Capsule ships complete · scorecard preserved.
